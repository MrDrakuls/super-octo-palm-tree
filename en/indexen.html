Journal Mission and Goals

Mission of the journal "Intellectuals" is to become the leading interdisciplinary platform for exchanging advanced scientific knowledge and ideas, fostering research integration and stimulating intellectual potential development in Russia and abroad. We aim to ensure wide, open access to high-quality scientific works that transcend the boundaries of traditional academic disciplines.

Journal Goal:

To contribute to the development of interdisciplinary theory and practice in a wide range of scientific fields, informing scholars, researchers, and practitioners about advanced fundamental and applied research, as well as achievements at the intersection of humanities, social, technical, and natural sciences.

Journal Objectives:

  • Communication Expansion: Forming and expanding an international communication space for collaboration between Russian and foreign specialists from various fields of knowledge.
  • Organizing Discussions: Providing an independent platform for organizing scientific debate and open discussions on current and controversial problems in science and practice.
  • High Standards: Ensuring independent scientific expertise and publishing results only from research that meets the highest standards of academic ethics and scientific reliability.
  • Supporting Young Scientists: Publishing research results from postgraduates, doctoral candidates, and degree applicants, facilitating their entry into the global scientific community.
  • Dissemination of Experience: Publishing materials from scientific conferences and symposiums, as well as promoting best practices for implementing scientific achievements in practice.
